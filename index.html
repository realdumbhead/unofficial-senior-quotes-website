What is the purpose of unofficial senior quotes and superlatives?

The school restricts what you can say on senior quotes and chooses the superlatives question, so I created a unofficial one where you can say anything you want on your quote and come up with any question for the superlative. That's right, you can say whatever you want and come up with your own superlatives.

Will the school know about it?

This is "unofficial" so I plan to keep it lowkey. This is has no relation with the school administration.

How does unofficial senior quotes work?

You enter a quote and your name in a form. I will take everybody's quote and create a document. After everyone enters his/her quote, I will print out the document and hand it out to everyone and release it online.

How does the superlatives work?

You submit your superlatives and then vote on your favorite superlatives. The top 15—maybe more if this becomes popular—superlatives will be chosen. After the voting process ends, you will be able to nominate people for the superlative.
